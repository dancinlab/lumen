# H_B3 — two-color narrow-arm litho

> **🜂 ABSTRACT.** Brainstorm shortlist (`state/resist-laser-brainstorm.md`, C3). Unverified.

## Which wall
Dose/flux — shift the energetic burden off EUV onto cheap photons.

## Principle
Resist reacts only where EUV AND a flood visible/IR beam overlap: the precise (narrow) EUV photon
"arms" the chemistry at the feature, the cheap flood "fires" it. EUV carries the resolution, not
the energy — so far fewer EUV photons are needed per feature.

## Falsifiable prediction
A two-color (EUV-arm + flood-develop) resist prints node CD at ≤ 20% the EUV photon dose of a
single-color resist, at equal CD/LER. If the flood step blurs the armed pattern beyond CD spec,
falsified.

## How it would be tested
Two-threshold exposure model (EUV arming density × flood completion) vs single-color dose; the
arming-pattern blur from the flood is the crux. Not run here.

## Honest status
Unverified (🜂); two-color/PEB-like schemes exist conceptually, the EUV-dose collapse is the claim.
Risk: armed-state lifetime and flood-induced blur.
