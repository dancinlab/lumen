## The @-question
Any @-partner must either (a) **make EUV photons** or (b) **change how many photons are needed**.
Supply splits exhaustively by extraction physics into three primitives; demand is a multiplier.

## Sharp findings
- **Only ONE family clears all walls** — the accelerator-coherent spine (H_022). The other three each
  fall on a *different* wall (flux / RLS-defect / CAPEX-redundant), so there is no second independent
  all-wall-clearing @. Robustness comes not from a backup family but from the demand-side multiplier
  composing with the spine.
- **Cooling is the clean lever; raising ρ is a TRAP.** Deeper cooling widens BOTH the FEL margin
  (ρ/σ) and the ERL margin (acceptance / √(σ²+ρ²)). Raising the Pierce ρ widens the FEL margin but
  *crushes* the ERL energy-acceptance margin — past a crossover ρ the post-FEL spread exceeds the ERL
  acceptance and energy recovery fails. → built as **H_024**.
- **Depletion:** the @-space is mapped (3 supply primitives + 1 demand multiplier); remaining ideas
  are variants. → declared in **H_025**.

## Sharpest open wall
The FEL beam-quality coupling (H_023) — addressed by deeper cooling (H_024), not by ρ. Exogenous to
the @-question, the campaign's true binding wall remains CAPEX/M7 (H_021); M8 waste-heat is ~0.5% (H_020).
